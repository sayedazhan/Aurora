# Aurora Vision

## Vision Statement

Aurora is the easiest way to send, split and settle money together.

Aurora combines payments, shared financial spaces and intelligent assistance into a single collaborative experience.

---

# Mission

Help people remove friction from shared money.

Whether you're:

- Living together
- Travelling together
- Running a club
- Organising an event
- Managing a family

Aurora makes shared finances simple.

---

# The Problem

Money is easy.

Managing money together is hard.

Today people use:

- Banking apps
- WhatsApp
- Excel
- Splitwise
- Notes
- Receipts

Aurora combines all of these experiences into one platform.

---

# Aurora's Core Principles

## 1. Payments First

Sending money should be effortless.

---

## 2. Collaboration First

Money always has context.

Every payment belongs somewhere.

Home.

Trip.

Family.

Club.

Business.

---

## 3. AI Assists

AI should remove work.

Never create work.

---

## 4. Mobile First

Aurora should feel amazing on mobile.

The web supports the mobile experience.

---

## 5. Security

Security is never optional.

Authentication.

Encryption.

Privacy.

Audit trails.

---

# Long-Term Vision

Aurora becomes the collaboration layer between people and money.

Banks move money.

Aurora helps people understand and manage it together.
