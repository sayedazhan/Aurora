# Executive Summary

Aurora is a collaborative financial platform that helps people send, split and settle money together.

Aurora combines modern payments, shared financial workspaces, AI assistance and real-time collaboration into a single experience.

Aurora is designed for:

- Friends
- Families
- Couples
- Housemates
- Clubs
- Small businesses

Aurora is **not** a bank.

Aurora orchestrates payments and collaboration.

---

# Product Vision

> Make shared money effortless.

---

# Mission

Help people manage money together with less friction, more transparency and intelligent assistance.

---

# MVP Scope

Aurora Version 1 includes:

- Authentication
- Workspace
- Financial Spaces
- Members
- Shared Expenses
- Settlements
- Payments
- Aurora Assist

Out of scope:

- Investments
- Loans
- Insurance
- Crypto
- Business accounting
- Advanced budgeting
